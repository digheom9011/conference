About ICACE 2025

Bringing together global experts to shape the future of civil engineering

Conference Overview

After two successful international conferences, ICACE 2025 brings together national and international researchers, scientists, and academicians to discuss the latest innovations in Civil Engineering.

The conference aims to advance knowledge in next-generation Civil Engineering by integrating innovation across multiple disciplines including Structural, Transportation, Environmental, and Water Resource Engineering.

This forum enables engineering graduates, academia and industry professionals to share innovations with special focus on sustainable research solutions to real-world problems.

About Sandip University

Sandip University, Nashik is a premier UGC recognized institution and the first self-financed private university in North Maharashtra, spread across 250 acres with state-of-the-art infrastructure.

Civil Engineering Department

The department is renowned for its industry-oriented approach, offering programs in B.Tech, M.Tech (various specializations), and Ph.D. in Civil Engineering, supported by experienced faculty and well-equipped laboratories.

Call for Papers

Contribute to the advancement of civil engineering knowledge

Abstract Submission

Authors are invited to submit abstracts of original research through email. Include:

  • Title and 250-300 word abstract
  • Author names and affiliations
  • Corresponding author contact details
  • 3-5 keywords
Submit Abstract

Full Paper Guidelines

After acceptance notification, submit full papers (6-8 pages) including:

  • Abstract and keywords
  • Introduction and methodology
  • Results with discussion
  • Conclusions and references
  • Proper formatting and citations
All accepted papers will be published in conference proceedings with ISBN
